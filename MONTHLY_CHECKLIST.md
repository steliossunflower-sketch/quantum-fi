## Daily Time Commitment

- **Minimum:** 30 minutes/day
- **Recommended:** 1-2 hours/day
- **Maximum:** Don't burn out! Quality over quantity.

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency is key** - Post regularly, even if it's small
2. **Engage with comments** - Respond to everyone who comments
3. **Share everywhere** - Don't be shy about promoting your work
4. **Be patient** - Building an audience takes time
5. **Have fun!** - If you're not enjoying it, take a break

---

## What to Do If You Fall Behind

- **Don't panic!** - This is a marathon, not a sprint
- **Prioritize** - GitHub and one blog post is better than nothing
- **Adjust timeline** - It's okay if it takes 6 weeks instead of 4
- **Ask for help** - Reach out to communities if you're stuck
